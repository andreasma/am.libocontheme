LibreOffice Conference
Berlin (Germany), 17–19/Oct/2012

 

The LibreOffice Conference is the annual gathering of the worldwide LibreOffice Community and interested developers, marketers, adopters, end-users, and supporters.

This year, Berlin (Germany) was the host for this important event.

The conference took place in the Conference Center of the Federal Ministry of Economics and Technology. For details, please refer to the page Venue.

Our program reflects the broad engagement and diversity of the community: it includes talks and workshops from various areas of the project. For details, please refer to the page Program.

To review the talks, refer to the presentation slides and video recordings.

 

Community Meetings

The so-called Community Meetings were held on Tuesday 16/Oct/2012, also in the rooms of the Federal Ministry of Economics and Technology in Berlin.

For details, please refer to the page Community Meetings.

 

The LibreOffice Conference 2012 is kindly supported by the German Federal Ministry of the Interior, and the German Federal Ministry of Economics and Technology.
